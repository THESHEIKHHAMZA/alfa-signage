ALFA Signage International was founded in 2016 with one conviction — every business deserves signage that truly speaks for its brand. We started as a precision fabrication workshop in India and have grown into a globally recognised signage manufacturing operation.

Today our 3D letter signs, LED logos and neon signs illuminate hotel lobbies, restaurant facades, motel forecourts and grocery storefronts across India, the USA, the UAE, the UK, Canada and Australia. Indian craftsmanship meets world-class manufacturing technology.

Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU
NEED TO KNOW.

Does ALFA Signage ship to the USA?

Yes. We ship to all US states using DHL and FedEx with full tracking and custom crating. We specialize in serving Indian-owned businesses including motels, restaurants and grocery stores across the USA. Delivery typically takes 5–10 business days after dispatch.

How much does a 3D letter sign cost?

3D letter signs start from approximately $299 USD for small signs. Pricing depends on size, material (stainless steel, acrylic, aluminium), illumination type and quantity. Contact us on WhatsApp at +91 8707098466 for a free custom quote — no commitment required.

Do you provide a free 3D design mockup?

Yes — completely free with every enquiry. Our designers create a photo-realistic 3D site visualization showing exactly how your sign will look on your building before any fabrication begins. Unlimited revisions until you are 100% satisfied.

How long does manufacturing and delivery take?

Standard production takes 10–15 working days after design approval. International shipping to USA, UK and UAE typically takes 5–10 business days via express courier. Total turnaround is usually 3–4 weeks from enquiry to delivery at your door.

Are your LED signs suitable for US outdoor weather?

Yes. All outdoor LED signs are IP65 or IP67 rated — fully weather-sealed for all US climates including rain, snow and extreme temperatures. Roadside pylon signs are wind-load engineered to meet US structural codes.

What types of businesses do you serve?

We serve motels, hotels, Indian restaurants, cafes, grocery stores, retail boutiques, corporate offices, hospitals, shopping malls and more. We have particular expertise with Indian-origin businesses in the USA, UAE and UK — understanding your brand identity and cultural aesthetics deeply.
